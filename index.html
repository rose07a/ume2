Skewer House on Pearl in Eugene
Mohawk Blvd in Springfield

This is my go to place. I love their rice! The sauce is delicious and they do a great job with their tofu as well. The plate portion is quite filling, I usually can only eat half and then save the other half. Their are 1-2 people that work the place. Can be a bit of a wait if there are a few people ahead of you but definitely worth the 10-15 min wait. They added a covered seating area to wait, which is very much appreciated :) Another plus, the staff are friendly and easy to talk to.

- Lindsey
